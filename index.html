Digital Development Networking Event at the Guardian

The Guardian News and Media

Wednesday, 11 November 2015 from 18:30 to 21:00 (GMT)

London, United Kingdom

The digital development team at The Guardian is working, in partnership with our editorial teams, to reinvent news for the digital and mobile future.

We are always looking for new talent to join us and have set up this event to allow you to network with others in the industry, meet the people behind the Guardian’s digital teams and learn what it’s like to work with us.

Members from across Engineering, QA, Product, UX and Data will be available to chat about what we are currently working on and our plans for the future. There will be a series of ‘lightning talks’ from our cross-functional streams to give you a taster of the sorts of challenges we face in the world of digital news.

The Guardian’s Women in Digital breakfast

Kings Cross

Wednesday, 2 December 2015 from 8.30am to 11am (GMT)

London, United Kingdom

The Guardian Digital Development department work in partnership with our editorial teams and are responsible for the technology behind our website, mobile apps, bespoke cms and a wide range of supporting services. We would like to welcome women working in the tech industry to come to and visit us in Kings Place and find out about how we are reinventing news for the digital and mobile future. More importantly we would like to invite women to come and join us in our project.

Over an informal breakfast we will hear from a panel of women from the team, who will introduce some of their work and discuss challenges for the future. There will be a chance for Q&A and for informal networking. You will be able to find out about all open positions.

If you would like to attend this event please email us – send us a link to your LinkedIn profile or CV or a letter telling us a bit about yourself – to sally.goble@theguardian.com
